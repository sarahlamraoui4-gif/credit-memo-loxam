# Credit Memo: Loxam Group

Independent credit analysis of Loxam Group, Europe's leading equipment 
rental company (€2.6bn revenue, 30 countries).

## Contents
- Investment recommendation: Senior Secured Unitranche at E+600bps
- Credit metrics: leverage (4.34x ND/EBITDA), ICR (4.1x), FCF (€260M)
- Risk framework: cyclicality, CAPEX intensity, geographic exposure
- Covenant structuring: ND/EBITDA <5.0x / ICR >2.5x
- Mitigants: asset backing, market leadership, sponsor support (CD&R)

## Skills demonstrated
Credit analysis, Covenant structuring, Leveraged credit and Risk assessment
